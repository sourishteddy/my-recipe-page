Brazilian Lemonade (Limonada Suíça)

A creamy, frothy, and incredibly refreshing drink from Brazil. Made with whole limes and sweetened condensed milk, it's a unique and delicious twist on classic lemonade that's ready in minutes!

Ingredients

  • 2 large, juicy limes
  • 4 cups of cold water
  • 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk (or to taste)
  • 1/4 cup sugar (optional, to taste)
  • Plenty of ice cubes for serving

Instructions

  1. Wash the limes thoroughly. Cut off the hard ends and slice each lime into 8 pieces.
  2. Place the lime pieces and 3 cups of the cold water into a blender.
  3. Pulse the blender 3 to 5 times for just a few seconds. Do not over-blend, or the drink will become too bitter from the white pith.
  4. Pour the mixture through a fine-mesh strainer into a pitcher to remove all the lime solids. Use a spoon to press the pulp and extract all the liquid.
  5. Discard the solids. Pour the strained lime liquid back into the (rinsed) blender.
  6. Add the sweetened condensed milk, the remaining 1 cup of water, and sugar (if using).
  7. Blend again for about 30 seconds until smooth and frothy.
  8. Fill glasses with ice cubes and pour the Brazilian Lemonade over top.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing taste!
